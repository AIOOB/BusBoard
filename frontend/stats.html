London Buses statistics

Summary

In the year 2016/17, there were 2.24 billion passenger journeys, and 300 million miles travelled. There are 673 routes (of which 52 are night buses), and 19,000 stops.

Factors affecting the fall in bus patronage

  • Congestion
  • Car ownership
  • Online shopping
  • Increase in tube usage

Vehicles

There are 10,000 buses in operations in London, 91% have CCTV, 97% have automatic vehicle location, and 98% have an accessibility certificate. Bus fares have increased by 66% between 2005 and 2017. 80% of buses were between 1 minute and up to 6 minutes early (on time).

Passengers

32% of bus journeys made in London were for commuting, compared to a 19% for England outside London.
